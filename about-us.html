A global business with a local expertise:

Petainer has eight manufacturing sites around the world to ensure we can produce and supply products as efficiently and sustainably as possible. We take a regionalized approach to our key markets in Europe, Russia, the Americas and Asia to ensure that our customers are supported as closely as possible with commercial, product and technical expertise.

We help businesses grow by increasing their flexibility, reducing their transportation and infrastructure costs, which ultimately results in a lower price pack per L.


We provide ready-to-use packaging solutions to regional and global brands or support them through our regional development and technical teams in setting up a local manufacture to blow our preform kits.

Green manufacturing
sites

We are proud to say that our European sites in Sweden and Czech Republic are now fully powered by Clean Energy essentially produced by wind and water power.

As a responsible global manufacturing business, we are not only striving to meet our customer’s business needs but are also committed to leading the way in sustainable innovation.

We regularly invest in new technologies and infrastructures to improve our supply chain and that of our customers. As an example, building a new warehouse next to our Czech site allowed us to remove over 1700 truck deliveries per year, a progress we are being able to track thanks to our carbon footprint calculator.

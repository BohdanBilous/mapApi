FAQ
Frequently Asked
Questions
Why Petainer
Kegs?

Petainer kegs help you access low rotation or new export markets and manage seasonal peaks. They increase your flexibility, reduce costs (no Capex), guarantee a superior product protection and are environmentally friendly.

01
How can Petainer kegs increase my revenue?

Petainer kegs create opportunities to explore new markets and increase exports. They are pre-purged with N2 and ready to be filled (or can be supplied in preform kits for local manufacture). They eliminate maintenance and return processes, and can generate up to 40% savings on logistics (versus steel kegs).

02
Where and how can I order kegs?

Please contact our sales team for further information.

Will my customers and I receive technical support?

Technical pre & after sales programs help customers and end-users in all regards, from customer training to the set-up of a full automatic blowing and filling lines.

01
Will the PET taint/affect the flavor of my product?

No, Petainer has contracted independent taste studies with trained taste panels that confirm that Petainer kegs do not affect the flavor of the beer or wine even after 12 months of storage. The studies also highlight that the PET taste results equal the results of stainless steel kegs.

02
Can I test the keg prior to ordering?

We provide free samples for testing purposes to qualifying brewers and beverage manufacturers or brand owners. Please contact our sales team for further information or find out more about our kegs compatibility.

In which countries are kegs available?

Petainer has global operations in Canada, US, Brazil, Sweden, Czech Rep, Russia, China, and Australia and services customers around the world. Please contact our sales team for further information.

01
Which keg volumes are available?

Our Hybrid kegs are available in 20 L and 30 L. Our Classic kegs are available in 10, 15, 20, 24 and 30 L. Discover our complete range of kegs.

02
Are Petainer kegs available at short notice?

Yes, Petainer kegs are available from stock. Our team can support you and help you react fast and handle sales peaks or unforeseen demands.

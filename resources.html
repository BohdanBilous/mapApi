01
Are Petainer kegs safe?

Yes, when using Petainer kegs according to our instructions and specifications, Petainer kegs are easy to handle and safe for products. Our kegs are available in two versions, with and without “pressure relief function”.

02
How to ?

For kegs with pressure relief function, the system is automatically depressurised at decoupling after dispensing. The Classic kegs are protected by outer packaging, either a cardboard or a polypropylene box. The Linestar and Hybrid kegs are additionally more stable by themselves.

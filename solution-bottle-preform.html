Post-consumer recycled resin.

We have been helping brands incorporate 25-50% post-consumer recyclate (PCR) flakes in their bottles since the 1990s and have the capability to supply food grade products with up to 100% PCR.

With our unique knowledge of processing of high quality contaminate-free PCR feedstock from Northern and Central Europe, we can provide world class sustainable preform solutions.

We have invested in production facilities to process responsibly sourced PCR flakes and pellets to guarantee consistent high quality products.

Preforms produced with PCR deliver high processability or increased line output whilst reducing energy and compressed air usage.

Petainer is the global leader in one—way PET kegs serving markets around the world, including Europe, Russia, North and South America and Asia. The petainerKeg range is tailored to the exacting needs of our customers offering versatility and flexibility, whilst providing superior economic and sustainability benefits.
Here’s Rafe Hutchings co-founder, commercial manager, of Jester Brewery, Santiago, Chile, which was founded in 2014, explaining why he is now using petainerKeg to help grow his business
We were receiving a huge amount of interest up and down Chile for our beer in draught, and we wanted to introduce our beer to regional Chilean cities. However, Chile is 4300km north to south so presents major logistical challenges.
Petainer has enabled us to open up new markets throughout Chile because it has made things simpler for us to transport our product. With PET, we have also reduced our carbon footprint while maintaining the quality and freshness of our beer. As a result, we are now using PET for all our draught beer clients outside our local market, Santiago.

4 photos Closing the loop.

How Petainer is taking recycling to the next level.

Since we started using petainerKeg, we now have new customers in Iquique, Antofagasta and La Serena in the north of Chile; the central coastal cities of Valparaiso and Viña del Mar, and in the south the cities of Concepcion, Valdivia, Pucon, Coyhaique, Puerto Varas and Punta Arenas too. North to south, these cities cover most of Chile´s 4300km length!
We have also received interest from importers in Europe, the USA and other Latin American countries, so we´ll consider exporting in the future with PET once we´ve achieved our home market goals. Chile is known for its excellent wine — it´d be fun to show people internationally that Chile has some great beers also!
We are very pleased to have been recognized by UNTAPPD as the best Chilean brewery in 2017 and all of 2018 so far. UNTAPPD represents the direct opinion of the drinker and that´s what we most value and are most proud of.

Injection Stretch Blow
Molding (ISBM)

Our two-stage production process offers multiple benefits (versus Extrusion blow molding: EBM).

We supply fully blown jars for short lead times or preforms for local blowing. Onsite production will greatly reduce your storage and transportation costs, while allowing you to blow jars on demand.
ISBM delivers a more consistent wall thickness and a smooth neck finish compared to EBM.
The versatility of blow molding onsite will deliver cost savings and offer more flexibility to your business.

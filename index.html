An innovation-driven
company.

With over 35 years of technical expertise, Petainer sets the benchmark in high-performance, sustainable packaging and works with world-leading brands across a wide range of food and beverage industries.

We continuously strive to improve the functional and environmental performance of our products. Our in-house scientists and development teams work with a worldwide network of leading institutions and suppliers to produce new products and barrier technologies. We strive to preserve and optimize resources by creating circular designs and transforming waste into a valuable resource.

@@include('./partials/elements/button-link.htm', { "text": "Learn More", "href" : "news.html#news-innovations", "classes": "btn-g btn-arrow-a btn-contact-us slide-btn" })

High-performance,
sustainable solutions.

Globally, Petainer is one of the leaders in sustainable innovation for food-grade PET beverage containers and one of the largest users of Post-Consumer Recyclate (PCR) in both refillable and single-use containers.

Our teams are dedicated to developing high-performance, sustainable packaging solutions in which we incorporate up to 100% recycled materials. Petainer is also leading the way in refillable bottles for the beverage industry, reducing the amount of virgin plastic material used per L.

expertise.

We offer a wide range of consulting and technical support services.

Our team consists of over 20 dedicated technical specialists, including polymer scientists, product designers, injection and blow molding specialists, as well as brewmasters and filling and dispense experts. We guide you through material selection, product design, and the blow molding process, providing training sessions and technical support.

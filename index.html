Innovation—driven
company.

With 35 years of technical expertise, Petainer sets the benchmark in sustainable high performance packaging across dozens of industries and sectors worldwide.

Combining business understanding with packaging innovation, we work with leading institutions and suppliers to develop new materials and barrier technologies to continuously improve the performance of our products.

A responsible
business

With our European sites already fully powered by clean energy, we are committed to reducing our and our customer’s carbon footprint by bringing the supply chain closer to the markets in which we operate.

Petainer has also proudly achieved a CSR Gold Rating by EcoVadis; which places our business in the top 1% of all Plastics Manufacturers evaluated by EcoVadis.

A local market
expertise.

With an Innovation Centre in the UK and over 8 global operations sites around the world, Petainer takes a regionalised approach to key markets in Europe, Russia, the Americas and Asia.

The expertise of our team is second to none and allows us to provide strong commercial and technical support to all our customers, while reducing costs and improving the product protection of world-leading brands.

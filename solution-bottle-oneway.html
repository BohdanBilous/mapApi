Material
Science.

Wehavebeenhelpingbrandsincorporate25-50%post- consumer recyclate (PCR) flakes in their bottles since the 1990s and have the capability to supply clear, high performance food grade products with up to 100% PCR.

With our unique knowledge of processing of high quality contaminate-free PCR feedstock from Northern and Central Europe, we can provide world class sustainable preform solutions.

We have invested in production facilities to process responsibly sourced PCR flakes and pellets to guarantee consistent high quality products.

We help brands achieve and exceed the Europeanregulations targets to incorporate 25% of recycledplastic in PET bottles as from 2025 and 30% in allplastic bottles as from 2030.
